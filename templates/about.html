About Me

Hello! I'm KyJahmy Phillip, a junior at Long Island University studying Computer Science. I am passionate about using music as a tool for mental wellness. This site explores the deep connection between music and mental health.

I've always been deeply passionate about music—not only as entertainment, but also as a hobby. Music can be used as a powerful tool for emotional and mental well-being. My personal experiences and academic research have shown me how deeply music can impact our moods, help us heal, and bring peace during difficult times.

Through this project, I aim to spread awareness about the link between music and mental health, share helpful tips, and showcase real-world examples that demonstrate music's incredible power to soothe the mind.

Why This Topic?

  • 💬 Music can reduce anxiety and stress.
  • 💬 Certain songs have scientifically proven therapeutic effects.
  • 💬 Everyone can find healing through music — no special skills required.
